1. Purpose of the Community
Our community is a safe, supportive, and inclusive space exclusively for LGBT+ people (lesbian, gay, bisexual, transgender, queer, and other members of the LGBT+ community).
We welcome only participants who identify as LGBT+. The community is created for sharing experiences, mutual support, friendship, discussing topics important to LGBT+ people, and fostering an atmosphere of acceptance and joy.
2. Core Principles

LGBT+-only space. Participation by individuals who do not identify as LGBT+ is not welcome and may result in removal. This includes cisgender heterosexual people (“allies”), even if they are supportive.
Respect for every participant’s self-identification.
The safety and comfort of LGBT+ people is the top priority.
Zero tolerance for homophobia, transphobia, bisexual erasure, or any form of LGBT+-phobia.

3. Expected Behavior

Show respect, kindness, and support toward other participants.
Use the correct names and pronouns indicated by each person.
Share your own experiences and listen to the experiences of others.
Respect confidentiality: do not share personal information of participants outside the community without explicit consent.
Be open to dialogue and constructive feedback.

4. Prohibited Behavior

Impersonation or hiding identity to gain access to the community (e.g., cisgender heterosexual people pretending to be LGBT+).
Any expressions of LGBT+-phobia, including jokes, stereotypes, microaggressions, or denial of LGBT+ experiences.
Harassment, discrimination, bullying, threats, or sexual misconduct.
Spam, off-topic posts, or advertising without moderator permission.
Sharing personal data of participants (doxxing).
Attempts to “debate” the legitimacy of LGBT+ identities.

5. Moderation and Consequences

Community moderators have the right to issue warnings, temporarily ban, or permanently remove participants for violations of this Code of Conduct.
Moderator decisions are final and not open for discussion.
If you witness a violation, please report it to the moderators.

6. Joining the Community
By joining the community, you confirm that:

You identify as an LGBT+ person;
You agree to this Code of Conduct;
You are ready to respect the boundaries and identities of other participants.

We are happy to welcome everyone who shares our values and is looking for a safe LGBT+ space
